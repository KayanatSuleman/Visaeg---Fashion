THE CONFIDENCE GUIDE; HOW TO DRESS YOUR BEST.

The first step to feeling your best, is to make sure you take the time to try which style clothes compliment your figure. The idea is to achieve the best ratio to your body, using clothes to compliment your curves.

 

The rule of thumb I like to follow when I dress myself is to make sure I pair loose fitting clothes with a tight fit, either a tight fit roll-neck complimented with loose fitting tailored trousers, give you the off duty look - by accentuating your natural body type.

 

It may seem obscure, but to think of your body in terms of ratio and proportions helps you balance clothing out, don't wear a shirt longer than your waist to give the illusion of longer legs, or wear a belt to accentuate your slim waist.
